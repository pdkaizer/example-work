Catholic Relief Services

The first of these samples is for a site my team developed for CRS' Advocacy unit. For this project, my team acted as the user experience consultant, but the site was designed and built by an outside agency. We developed the IA, sitemap and wireframes. The final live site can be seen at: http://www.confrontglobalpoverty.org/

The second sample is a site we built for CRS' fundraising team for a community fundraising and meal-packing program. We created the the entire site for this project, including IA, site design and custom Wordpress theme development. The final live site can be seen at: http://helpinghands.crs.org.
