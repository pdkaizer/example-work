Creative Brief & Strategy:

Here are two documents that I feel are good examples of strategic writing. Both are sections of larger documents the Catholic Relief Services marketing and communications leadership group—of which I was a member—produced for the new CEO of CRS.

The first is a strategic assessment of the opportunities for marketing and communications for CRS. I wrote the Web/Multimedia section.

As a follow up to the first document, the CEO asked the marketing team to produce what was essentially a creative brief focused on what A-level work would look like for CRS. On a side note that the new CEO of CRS came to CRS from a lifetime in academia, So she tended to use grades to evaluate our work. I wrote the digital section.

Catholic Relief Services

The first of these samples is for a site my team developed for CRS' Advocacy unit. For this project, my team acted as the user experience consultant, but the site was designed and built by an outside agency. We developed the IA, sitemap and wireframes. The final live site can be seen at: http://www.confrontglobalpoverty.org/

The second sample is a site we built for CRS' fundraising team for a community fundraising and meal-packing program. We created the the entire site for this project, including IA, site design and custom Wordpress theme development. The final live site can be seen at: http://helpinghands.crs.org.
